About

Phildmon Mhondow was born in Zambia. He came to the United Stated at the age of 7. Ever since then, this country has become his home. While here, he has lived in 3 different states, Illinois, Minnesota, and Nebraska. He is a down to earth person who always see the glass half full instead of it being half empty. If you ask most of his friends, they’ll say that he has a great personality. As far as ideology on life, he believes that human being wasn’t place in this world by themselves. That we were created by an intellectual being. Basically, he is a Christian who believe in God.

For hobbies, Phildmon does numerous things. He Play basketball, go to the gym, cook, collect African arts, and share his faith through passing out literatures. To provide for his daily needs he lives an entrepreneurial lifestyle. Before he started this entrepreneurial adventure, he was working in corporate America as a health Insurance sales representative. It is in this job that he achieved one of his great accomplishments. His company which was in Minnesota at that time wanted to open an office branch in Nebraska. He was selected to spear headed it. In this task he was required to hire, train new sale employees and make sure that the office was running smoothly.

Not only does Phildmon have career accomplishments, but he also has personal achievements too. One of the most exhilarated personal achievements he’s proud of was sky diving. He went sky diving in 2015. He will never forget it because that moment was captured in numerous pictures. Another personal goal he achieved was going to United Kingdom. This was something he wanted to do for a while. He did it in 2018 and stayed there for a whole month. Overall Phidmon Mhondow is an outgoing person who is very friendly and is loved by everyone who knows him.
